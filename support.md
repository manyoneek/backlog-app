## FAQ

**Q: Where is my data stored?**  
A: All your data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to any server.

**Q: Can I sync my library across devices?**  
A: Not yet, but we're exploring iCloud sync for a future update.

**Q: Where does the game information come from?**  
A: Game details, ratings, and images come from [RAWG](https://rawg.io/), one of the largest video game databases.

**Q: Is the app free?**  
A: Yes. No ads, no subscriptions, no in-app purchases.
